Thank you for calling the Town of Henrietta Building and Fire Prevention Department. If you are calling to schedule an inspection, you must speak to a team member. Please do not leave a message with your request. To speak with a team member to schedule an inspection, press 1. To speak with a fire marshal, press 2. For current or proposed commercial building projects, new home builds, or questions regarding zoning, press 3. For questions regarding proposed residential home projects or sign permits, press 4.
