Thank you for calling the Greece Public Library. Our service hours are as follows. Monday through Thursday, the library will be open from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. Friday and Saturday, the library will be open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Please note that the library will open at 12 p.m. on the first Thursday of each month. Please press 3 to speak with a librarian during our open hours.
