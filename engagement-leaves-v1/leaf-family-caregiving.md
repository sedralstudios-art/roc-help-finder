# Caring for an older parent or disabled adult

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Family — kids, breakup, caregiving, baby on the way**
2. _Which best fits your situation?_ → **Caring for an older parent or disabled adult**

EISEP is the in-home help program most people miss. Office for the Aging is the front door for seniors.

## What to look at

### ofa

Office for the Aging — meals, transportation, Medicare help, caregiver support

_Type: program_

### eisep

Free in-home help for seniors 60+ NOT on Medicaid — personal care + housekeeping

_Type: program_

### mow

Meals on Wheels — hot meals delivered if 60+ or homebound

_Type: program_
