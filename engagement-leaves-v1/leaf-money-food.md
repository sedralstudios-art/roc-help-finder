# Not enough food in the house

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Money is tight or bills are piling up**
2. _Which of these is the biggest pain right now?_ → **Not enough food in the house**

Several things stack on top of each other — most don't require an income check.

## What to look at

### snap

Monthly food benefit on an EBT card

_Type: program_

### snap-rights-ny

What rights SNAP recipients have — recertification, fair hearings, post-conviction eligibility

_Type: entry_

### foodlink

Free pantry — no paperwork, no income check at most locations

_Type: program_

### wic

If pregnant, postpartum, or kids under 5 — extra free food + nutrition counseling

_Type: program_

### meals

If RCSD student — free breakfast and lunch automatically

_Type: program_

### market

Rochester Public Market — Double Up matches SNAP $1-for-$1 on produce

_Type: program_

### mow

Meals on Wheels if 60+ or homebound

_Type: program_

Didn't see what you need? Try the [main HelpFinder questionnaire](/help) for more specific situations, or call **211** for staffed routing.
