# Pregnant or new baby

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Family — kids, breakup, caregiving, baby on the way**
2. _Which best fits your situation?_ → **Pregnant or new baby**

Several stacking benefits — and NY's new Paid Prenatal Leave is unique.

## What to look at

### wic

WIC — free healthy food + nutrition counseling for pregnancy + kids under 5

_Type: program_

### medicaid

Pregnancy Medicaid available even at higher incomes; kids under 1 covered automatically

_Type: program_

### chip

Child Health Plus — kids under 19, scales with income

_Type: program_
