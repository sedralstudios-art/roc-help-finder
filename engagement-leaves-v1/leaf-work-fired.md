# Got fired or laid off

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Something at work or losing my job**
2. _Which one is closest?_ → **Got fired or laid off**

Some firings are unlawful even in an at-will state. Filing has hard deadlines.

## What to look at

### wrongful-termination-ny

When a firing is against the law — discrimination, retaliation, whistleblower

_Type: entry_

### ta

Temporary Assistance to bridge until next paycheck

_Type: program_

### snap

Apply right away — SNAP processes faster after job loss

_Type: program_

### medicaid

Lost-job often qualifies for Medicaid even if it didn't before

_Type: program_
