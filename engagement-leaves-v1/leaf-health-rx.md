# Can't afford prescriptions

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Health, doctor, or medication problem**
2. _What's the most pressing health issue?_ → **Can't afford prescriptions**

Three things stack here — and the state has a senior-specific program most people miss.

## What to look at

### nyrxcard

Free discount card — up to 80% off, no application, use at CVS or Wegmans

_Type: program_

### epic

EPIC — NY's prescription program for seniors 65+ on Medicare

_Type: program_

### trill

Trillium pharmacy — sliding scale, no insurance required

_Type: program_
