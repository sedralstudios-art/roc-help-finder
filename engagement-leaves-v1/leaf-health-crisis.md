# Mental health crisis or addiction

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Health, doctor, or medication problem**
2. _What's the most pressing health issue?_ → **Mental health crisis or addiction**

> ⚠ **If suicide or immediate self-harm: call or text 988 — free, 24/7. Spanish text: AYUDA to 988.**

If in immediate danger to self or others, call 988 right now or go to a Strong walk-in. Otherwise these are the next-best paths.

## What to look at

### 988crisis

Suicide and crisis lifeline — 24/7, includes addiction crisis

_Type: program_

### strongcrisis

Strong Memorial Psych Emergency — walk-in 24/7, no appointment

_Type: program_

### bhacccrisis

BHACC — walk-in crisis center, no appointment, daily 9am-9pm

_Type: program_

### trill

Trillium Health behavioral health — sliding scale

_Type: program_
