# Landlord won't return my security deposit

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Where I live — eviction, repairs, no place to go**
2. _Which is happening right now?_ → **Landlord won't return my security deposit**

NY caps deposits at 1 month and gives a 14-day return deadline. Missing the deadline forfeits the right to keep ANY of it.
