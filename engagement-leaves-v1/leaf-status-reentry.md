# Recently released from incarceration

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Something about my situation — record, immigration, age, disability**
2. _Which is closest?_ → **Recently released from incarceration**

Reentry has multiple stacking supports. SNAP and Medicaid often re-activate post-release.

## What to look at

### clean-slate-ny

Most older convictions seal automatically under Clean Slate Act

_Type: entry_

### snap

SNAP after conviction — most felony bars eliminated under recent NY changes

_Type: program_
