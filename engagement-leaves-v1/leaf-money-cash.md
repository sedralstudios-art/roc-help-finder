# Need cash now and don't know where to start

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Money is tight or bills are piling up**
2. _Which of these is the biggest pain right now?_ → **Need cash now and don't know where to start**

There's no single 'just give me money' program, but a few benefits add up. The state's myBenefits portal is the one application that covers most.

## What to look at

### myb

One application — SNAP + Medicaid + cash assistance + HEAP + childcare in one

_Type: program_

### ta

Temporary Assistance — monthly cash for rent, utilities, basics

_Type: program_

### eitc

Earned Income Tax Credit — refundable, you may have unclaimed past years

_Type: program_

### vita

CASH/VITA does free tax prep — can help recover past EITC

_Type: program_

### benefits-verification-letter-ny-program

How to prove SNAP/Medicaid/cash receipt to landlord, employer, court — uniform letter from each agency

_Type: entry_
