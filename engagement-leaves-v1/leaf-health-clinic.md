# Need a doctor / dentist and can't pay

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Health, doctor, or medication problem**
2. _What's the most pressing health issue?_ → **Need a doctor / dentist and can't pay**

Sliding-scale clinics see anyone who walks in — Jordan and Trillium take everyone insured or not.

_Type: program_

_Type: program_

_Type: program_
