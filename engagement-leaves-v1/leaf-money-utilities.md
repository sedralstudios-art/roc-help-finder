# Behind on heat / electric / gas

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Money is tight or bills are piling up**
2. _Which of these is the biggest pain right now?_ → **Behind on heat / electric / gas**

Utility shutoff has a specific path — and there's a separate program for the equipment itself.

## What to look at

### heap

Help with heating bills; emergency HEAP if heat is shut off

_Type: program_

### herr

If 60+ and own the home — pays up to $4-8K to repair or replace the furnace

_Type: program_

### rge

RG&E payment plans — call BEFORE shutoff

_Type: program_

### empower

Free home insulation, air sealing, heat pumps — lowers future bills

_Type: program_
