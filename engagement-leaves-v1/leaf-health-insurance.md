# No insurance / lost insurance

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Health, doctor, or medication problem**
2. _What's the most pressing health issue?_ → **No insurance / lost insurance**

Most NY adults under 65 apply through NY State of Health. Different doors for different situations.

## What to look at

### medicaid

Free or very low cost — covers doctors, hospital, dental, mental health, prescriptions

_Type: program_

### chip

If kids under 19 — free or low-cost coverage with a per-income premium

_Type: program_

### myb

One application covers Medicaid + cash + SNAP + HEAP

_Type: program_
