# Can't pay rent or behind on rent

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Money is tight or bills are piling up**
2. _Which of these is the biggest pain right now?_ → **Can't pay rent or behind on rent**

Behind on rent has more options than most people realize. Here's a starting list — none of these require a lawyer.

## What to look at

### ta

Emergency rent / one-shot deal at Monroe County DSS — apply in person at 691 St Paul

_Type: program_

### communityplace

Rent assistance + case management; specific zips

_Type: program_

### eviction-process-ny

What the landlord has to do BEFORE eviction — buys time and may surface defenses

_Type: entry_

### tenant-habitability-ny

Rent-withholding may be available if the unit has serious problems the landlord won't fix

_Type: entry_

### 211

Call 211 — they coordinate emergency rental assistance and can route to current programs

_Type: program_
