# I have a criminal record from years ago

**Path through the questionnaire:**

1. _What's the biggest thing on your mind right now?_ → **Something about my situation — record, immigration, age, disability**
2. _Which is closest?_ → **I have a criminal record from years ago**

NY's Clean Slate Act now seals most older convictions automatically — most people don't realize they qualify.

## What to look at

### clean-slate-ny

Anchor — automatic sealing of older convictions; nothing to file

_Type: entry_

### wjcny

Worker Justice Center — represents people with records facing employment discrimination

_Type: program_

### snap-rights-ny

SNAP eligibility after a conviction — most felony bars eliminated under recent NY changes

_Type: entry_

### 211

Call 211 — connects to current reentry programs in Monroe County

_Type: program_
