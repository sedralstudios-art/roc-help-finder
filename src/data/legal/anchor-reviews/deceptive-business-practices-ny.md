## R3 — 2026-05-01 (1 substantive concern + treble-phrasing tightening)

**SUBSTANTIVE:** the FAIR Act $15k / 3x-restitution penalty paragraph was
REMOVED. Covington & Burling (Dec 23 2025) explicitly noted the
higher-penalty provision did not survive into the enacted version. Sources
conflict (Scarinci Hollenbeck still cites the figure post-enactment).
**Pending direct attorney verification against Chapter 708 of 2025 (S8416)
enacted text.** Existing $5k / violation penalty under GBL § 350-d remains
accurate.

**PHRASING:** treble damages clause rewritten with explicit example math:
- Actual damages $200 → bonus $400 → total $600.
- Actual $5,000 → bonus capped at $1,000 → total $6,000.

So it cannot be misread as "actual + 3× actual + $1,000." Same fix applied
to the HVAC anchor's GBL § 349 paragraph.
