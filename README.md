# ROC Help Finder

Free resource finder for Rochester, NY and Monroe County.

**130+ programs · 29 categories · 8 languages · Zero data collected**

Food, housing, heating, health insurance, jobs, legal help, addiction recovery, domestic violence support, refugee services, veteran services, LGBTQ+ support, and more.

## What this does

A person answers 3 simple questions. The app shows them every program they may qualify for — with phone numbers, addresses, hours, what documents to bring, and direct links to apply online.

## Privacy

This tool collects **absolutely nothing**. No names, no IP addresses, no cookies, no tracking, no analytics. Everything runs on the user's device. When they close the page, everything disappears.
